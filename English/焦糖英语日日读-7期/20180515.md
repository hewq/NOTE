# 邂逅顶级大餐

## 询问点餐

- What can I get you?

  > 你们要来点什么？

- I will have xxx?

  > 我想吃xxx

## 西式大餐小知识

- main course

  > 主菜

- starter

  > 开胃菜

- dessert

  > 甜点

## 客人点餐

- `I'd like to start with` the chicken soup.

  > 我想先来一份鸡汤

- `I'd like` to have a conversation `with` you.

  > 我想和你谈话。

- `Please wait in line` like everyone else.

  > 请像别人一样排队等候。
